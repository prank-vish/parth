About Wood Craft

Wood Craft is a traditional art form of carving and crafting wood into beautiful and functional pieces. It is known for its intricate designs and skilled craftsmanship.

Wood Craft artisans use various types of wood to create furniture, decorative items, and other artifacts that reflect the rich cultural heritage of the region.

This craft form has been passed down through generations and continues to be a vital part of the cultural identity of the region.

Styles and Techniques

Wood Craft involves the use of various types of wood to create intricate designs and patterns. Artisans use traditional tools and techniques to carve and shape the wood into beautiful and functional pieces.

History and Significance

Wood Craft has its roots in the rich cultural traditions of various regions. It has gained recognition and appreciation both in India and internationally for its unique style and cultural significance.

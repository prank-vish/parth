About Sarso Ka Saag

Sarso Ka Saag is a traditional Punjabi dish made from mustard greens and spices. It is usually served with Makki Ki Roti (corn flatbread) and is a winter delicacy in Northern India.

Ingredients

The main ingredients for Sarso Ka Saag are mustard greens, spinach, bathua (goosefoot), and spices such as ginger, garlic, green chilies, and maize flour for thickening. It is traditionally served with a dollop of butter or ghee.

History

Sarso Ka Saag has been a staple in Punjabi cuisine for centuries. It is particularly popular during the winter months when mustard greens are in season. The dish reflects the rich agricultural heritage of Punjab.

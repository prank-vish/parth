About Chole Bhature

Chole Bhature is a popular North Indian dish made of spicy chickpeas (chole) and deep-fried bread (bhature). It is a favorite breakfast or brunch item, especially in Punjab and Delhi.

Ingredients

The key ingredients for Chole Bhature include chickpeas, tomatoes, onions, spices, and flour. The chickpeas are cooked in a rich, spicy gravy, and the bhature is made from a dough of flour, yogurt, and baking powder.

History

Chole Bhature has its origins in the Punjab region of India and Pakistan. It has become a popular dish across India, known for its rich flavors and hearty composition.

About Dosa

Dosa is a popular South Indian dish made from fermented rice and lentil batter. It's a crispy, savory pancake often served with chutneys and sambar.

Back to Cuisine Try Quiz

Overview

Dosa is known for its crisp texture and delicious flavor. It's a versatile dish that can be enjoyed plain or with various fillings, and is commonly served with a range of accompaniments.

Significance

Dosa is a staple of South Indian cuisine and has become popular throughout India and the world. It reflects the rich culinary traditions of the region and is enjoyed by people of all ages.

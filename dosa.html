About Dosa

Dosa is a traditional South Indian dish made from fermented rice and urad dal batter, cooked into a thin, crispy pancake. It is often served with sambar and coconut chutney.

Ingredients

The key ingredients for Dosa are rice, urad dal (black gram), fenugreek seeds, and water. The batter is fermented overnight and then spread thin on a hot griddle to make crispy dosas.

History

Dosa has its origins in South India and has been a staple food in the region for centuries. It is now enjoyed across India and globally for its unique taste and texture.
